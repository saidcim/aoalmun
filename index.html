About

Diplomacy in its
purest form

AOALMUN — the Assembly of Aspiring Leaders Model United Nations — is a premier conference designed to challenge, inspire, and elevate the next generation of global thinkers and decision-makers.

"In the chamber of debate, ideas become policy, and students become statesmen."

We bring together delegates from diverse backgrounds to tackle the world's most pressing issues through rigorous debate, collaborative resolution-writing, and the timeless art of negotiation.

Deliberation & Debate

Our
Committees

AOALMUN convenes four distinguished committees, each tasked with addressing critical issues at the forefront of international discourse. Delegates will be assigned based on preferences and experience level.

UNSC United Nations Security Council Advanced
+

The Security Council bears primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security. Delegates represent the P5 and elected members, exercising some of the most powerful diplomatic authority in the simulation.

Agenda Items
I. The Situation in the South China Sea: Maritime Sovereignty and Regional Stability
II. Counter-Terrorism Frameworks and the Protection of Civilian Populations

UNGA UN General Assembly — First Committee Intermediate
+

The First Committee of the General Assembly deals with disarmament, global challenges, and threats to peace that affect the international community. All 193 UN member states are represented, making it one of the most dynamic diplomatic arenas.

Agenda Items
